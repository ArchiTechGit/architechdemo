## Design Context

### Users
Enterprise buyers and technical stakeholders (often in critical infrastructure / healthcare) reviewing ArchiTech's Webex Contact Centre demos before or during a sales conversation. This homepage is the first thing they see — a table of contents to the demo suite, not a marketing page. Job to be done: find and open the right demo fast.

### Brand Personality
Confident / authoritative, precise / technical, warm / human. A trusted partner in high-stakes environments — not a startup, not cold/corporate either. Full brand voice and visual system is documented in `brand_assets/BRAND_GUIDELINES.md` (source of truth for colors, motion rules, voice).

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme, ArchiTech's documented color tokens are fixed (`#0D1825` bg, `#05C3DD` cyan, `#0055B8` blue, `#7F8FA9`/`#3D4F65` text) — do not introduce new colors. Typography is open for this homepage specifically: a distinctive display/body pairing is welcome instead of defaulting to Roboto, as long as it reads confident/precise/warm rather than generic. No specific visual reference chosen — use judgment. Avoid the AI-slop defaults (cyan-glow-on-black cliché, gradient text, glassmorphism, card grids, hero-metric templates).

### Design Principles
1. Brand color tokens are non-negotiable; typography and layout are the creative levers on this page.
2. One well-executed signature moment beats scattered decoration — pick a single memorable detail and execute it precisely.
3. Every element must earn enterprise credibility or functional clarity — nothing purely decorative (per brand doc).
4. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; keep motion purposeful (state changes, entrances), never bouncy or gratuitous.
5. This is a navigation page first — legibility and fast task completion (find your demo, click it) always outrank visual flourish.
