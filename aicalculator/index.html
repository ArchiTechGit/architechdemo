How this is calculated
PoolVoice capacity / unitDigital capacity / unit

AI Agent — Autonomous250 min200 sessions

Voice minutes = avg calls/month × avg minutes/call. A digital session = up to 10 messages. Fractional unit consumption for a tier = the greater of (voice minutes ÷ voice capacity) and (digital sessions ÷ digital capacity) — each unit covers both dimensions simultaneously, so voice and digital usage share the same unit rather than needing separate units. Scripted and Autonomous are the same AI Agent SKU, so their fractional consumption is summed before rounding up — e.g. 0.3 Scripted + 0.3 Autonomous = 1 unit, not 2. AI Assistant is a separate SKU and is rounded independently. Costs and committed-order pricing are deliberately excluded — see the sales deck for RRP.
