How this is calculated
PoolVoice capacity / unitDigital capacity / unit

Voice minutes = avg calls/month × avg minutes/call. A digital session = up to 10 messages. Units required for a pool = the greater of (voice minutes ÷ voice capacity) and (digital sessions ÷ digital capacity), rounded up — each unit covers both dimensions simultaneously, so voice and digital usage share the same unit rather than needing separate units. Scripted and Autonomous are purchased as separate unit pools and are summed for the AI Agent total. Costs and committed-order pricing are deliberately excluded — see the sales deck for RRP.
